# SuperviseAI 2-Minute Demo Script

## 0:00-0:15 Intro

Today we are presenting SuperviseAI, an AI-powered thesis supervision and viva coaching platform for universities.

## 0:15-0:35 Problem

Thesis supervision is manual and fragmented. Professors juggle many students, feedback is delayed, and students often face defense with limited structured practice.

## 0:35-1:05 Student Flow

Show student workspace.

- Create thesis proposal.
- Upload a PDF or DOCX draft.
- Show processing stages updating in realtime.
- Show results: progress score, citation issues, plagiarism status, and next actions.

Narration:
With one upload, the student gets immediate, structured guidance.

## 1:05-1:35 Professor Flow

Show professor dashboard.

- Open cohort overview with risk and progress signals.
- Open one student detail page.
- Show version comparison, timeline, and report summaries.

Narration:
Professors can quickly see who needs intervention and why.

## 1:35-1:55 AI Coaching Flow

Show Mock Viva / Argument Defender.

- Start session.
- Show adaptive question/response behavior.
- Highlight live metrics and final readiness score.

Narration:
The AI coach adapts by confidence and sentiment, simulating real defense pressure.

## 1:55-2:00 Close

SuperviseAI shortens feedback cycles, improves thesis quality, and helps students defend with confidence.
